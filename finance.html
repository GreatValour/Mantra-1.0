      Overview

      As a Finance Intern joining us full-time remotely, you will play a crucial role in gaining hands-on experience in financial transaction management with professionalism and precision. Your responsibilities will include learning and assisting in handling cash and credit/debit card payments, delivering outstanding customer service, and ensuring a seamless payment experience. Additionally, you will have the opportunity to contribute to maintaining accurate transaction records, facilitating returns and exchanges, and gaining exposure to inventory management as part of your daily tasks.

      Responsibilities

      • Will learn accounts payable and accounts receivable.
      • Handle Domestic and international invoices.
      • Participate in projects including but not limited to: Cost savings, Payment Gateways, POS systems and many more.
      • Resolve any payment-related issues or discrepancies
      • Assist with payment gateways and systems, POS systems, and Cash applications.

      This job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. Duties, responsibilities, and activities may change at any time with or without notice.

      Qualifications

      • Currently enrolled in a high school program or equivalent: Actively pursuing a high school diploma or equivalent educational qualification.

      Job-related experience

      • Interest in finance and cash handling: A background or strong interest in finance, coupled with an eagerness to learn about managing financial transactions.
      • Excellent customer service and communication skills: The ability to provide top-notch customer service, ensuring a positive experience for every patron.
      • Attention to detail and accuracy in record-keeping: A keen eye for detail, enabling you to maintain precise and error-free transaction records.
      • Ability to work independently and remotely: Demonstrated capability to thrive in a remote work environment while efficiently carrying out your responsibilities.
      • Flexibility in working hours: Availability to work flexible hours, including weekends and holidays, to meet the diverse needs of the business.
      • Interest or coursework in finance (a plus): Previous exposure or coursework in finance would be advantageous.
      • Currently pursuing a degree in finance or related field: Actively enrolled in a finance-related program, showcasing your commitment to a career in finance.

      • Proficient in MS-Excel, PowerPoint, and Word.
      • Effective communication and interpersonal skills.
      • Effective time management skills.
